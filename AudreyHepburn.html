Audrey Hepburn

Audrey Hepburn was born May 4, 1929 in Ixelles, Belgium. Her birth name was Audrey Kathleen Ruston Her mothers name was Ella van Heemstra and her fathers name was Joseph Victor Ruston. Her parents divorced and Audrey went and moved to London with her mother were she attended private school. When she vacationed in Arnhem, Netherlands with her mother, Hitler took over the town. This is were Audrey suffered from depression and malnutrition. After all these hard times Audrey Hepburn went to ballet school in London and began her modeling career.
