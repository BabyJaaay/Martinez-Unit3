Audrey Hepburn

Audrey Hepburn was born May 4, 1929 in Ixelles, Belgium. Her birth name was Audrey Kathleen Ruston. Her mothers name was Ella van Heemstra and her fathers name was Joseph Victor Ruston. Her parents divorced and Audrey went and moved to London with her mother were she attended private school. When she vacationed in Arnhem, Netherlands with her mother, Hitler took over the town. This is were Audrey suffered from depression and malnutrition. After all these hard times Audrey Hepburn went to ballet school in London and began her modeling career. Audrey wasn't a sex symbol in America but more of an innocence and class which gained her a lot of very devoted fans. Audrey was married twice; Mel Ferrer from September 25, 1954 - December 5, 1968 and had one child, Andrea Dotti from January 18, 1969 - September 21, 1982 also had one child with him. Audrey Hepburn became an ambassador of the UNICEF, helping Latin American and African children in 1988.
