Elizabeth Taylor

Elizabeth Rosemond Taylor was born February 27, 1932 in Hampstead, London, England in the United Kingdom. The one special and most captivating physical appeal were her violet eyes who the majority of the time attracted an audience. Her parents, both American, Sara Sothern and Francis Lenn Taylor, St. Louis, Missouri art dealers. When Elizabeth was 10 years old she got signed for a contract, her first movie was "There's One Born Every Minute" in 1942. After that Elizabeth Taylor began to appear film after film until 1985 were she began crusades for AIDS sufferers. Also, in 1959 Elizabeth turned to Judaism and identified herself a Jew all throughout the rest of her life. Taylor was married eight times, no husband longer than 10 years together. All spouse were divorced except Michael Todd, who died in a a car accident in 1958. She had 4 children, two with Michael Wilding, one with Michael Todd and another with Richard Burton.
