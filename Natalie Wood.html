Natalie Wood

Natalie Wood, birth name Natalia Nikolaevna Zakharenko, was born on July 20, 1938 in San Francisco, California. Her parents both Russian-born, Maria Stepanovna and Nikolai Stepanovna Zakharenko, who changed their last name to Gurdin after they became US citizens. 1946 was the year for Natalie when she was only seven years old, she screen tested twice because the first time wasn't her luck. From the late 1940's and early 1950's Elizabeth was very busy, appearing in about 18 films. Nominated for many of her films that she had participated on, her small petite frame gave her chances to play vulnerable characters in films. Natalie Wood was married three times; Robert Wagner from December 28, 1957 - April 27, 1962, Richard Gregson from May 30, 1969 - April 12, 1972 and Robert Wagner from July 16, 1972 - November 29, 1981.
